How AI helps SEO

When it comes to improving your website's search engine optimization, AI written content can be a powerful tool. Not only can AI written content help you to target your audience more effectively, but it can also help to improve your overall website's appearance.


By using AI written content, you can ensure that your website's content is both relevant and engaging. This can help to improve your website's overall usability and ranking within search engines.

How AI writing works

AI writing works by taking a piece of text and breaking it down into individual words, sentences, and paragraphs.


Once the text is broken down into its smallest parts, a computer can then start to analyze it and identify the different words and phrases that are most important. After the computer has identified the most important words and phrases, it can then start to generate new text based on those keywords.
